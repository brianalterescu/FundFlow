# FundFlow
All-in-One Social Finance Tracker

FundFlow is a modern, web-based finance management application designed to make group and personal money tracking simple, transparent, and collaborative. Unlike traditional budgeting tools that focus only on individual spending, FundFlow blends professional-grade financial analytics with social features that encourage accountability and fairness among friends, roommates, and teams.

With Google Sign-In integration, users can securely access their dashboard, track group expenses, monitor savings goals, and analyze spending trends. FundFlow highlights both individual insights (like category-based spending, monthly savings progress) and group dynamics (who owes what, shared budgets, fairness scores).

The platform goes beyond static reports by adding social features such as commenting on expenses, friendly reminders, goal sharing, and gamification — making financial responsibility more engaging and less stressful.
